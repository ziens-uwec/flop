General Info

Use & Relevancy

Floppy discs are still used in the music industry, loading various softwares and being used by several common pieces of hardware. Floppy discs are also still used in the aviation industry, to load data into some models of plane.

Shipping Info

Flop can ship to most countries outside the US using airmail, however it may take up to a month. Most items ship in the US by ground and arrive within 10 business days. Flop does not offer free shipping at this time.

Return Policy

Anything can be returned within 1 week of delivery with a full refund for any reason. Products are warrantied for up to 2 months after delivery, and can be returned and replaced for any damage not caused by misuse. Returns from outside the US will pay for the cost to ship products back.
